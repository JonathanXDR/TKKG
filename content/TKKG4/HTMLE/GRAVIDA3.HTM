Friday, January 17th 1969
PALM Works call in receivers



On Monday, Mr Gradiva, owner and managing director of the PALM works, called in the official receivers. At the press conference, Mr Gradiva explained:
"For many years, we led the market in men´s and boy´s clothing. Now, it seems that our conservative style is no longer in demand. The PALM works are closing their gates and the Gradiva family is withdrawing from the textile manufacturing business."
Many a tear was shed at a meeting of workers when Mr Gradiva announced the immediate closure of the plant.

"We´ve always been like a family", Simone Dyer (53), a seamstress at the factory from the age of 15, said. Well-informed sources are quoted as saying that takeover negotiations with market leader C&B Brinkelhuber have already started. A result is expected at the beginning of next week.
