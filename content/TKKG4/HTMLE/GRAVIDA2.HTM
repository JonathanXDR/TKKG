Tuesday, June 20th 1961
Mysterious Disappearance on the Lake



"It´s as if the earth had opened up and swallowed Eric whole", said inspector March yesterday at the police´s special unit press conference about their investigations into Eric´s disappearance.

Until now, police have found no trace of the kidnappers. Police units have been asking neighbours on the lake. People paddling and using sailing boats have been asked to report to the police. But no-one saw anything suspicious. The police are assuming that this is the work of a gang of highly specialised criminals.

The location had been so carefully reconnoitred in advance that the kidnappers were able to pull off the crime completely undetected. "This is undoubtedly the work of professionals", said Marsh in conclusion. He is hoping that, with the help of Interpol, an exact criminal profile can be put together in the next few days.
